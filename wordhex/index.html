Practice & Modes

SpellCast supports quick solo drills or full multiplayer lobbies. Hop into Practice to play against adaptive bots or launch a New Game to invite your Discord server. Every match runs for five 30-second rounds and the highest total score wins.

  • Practice: Solo queue with adjustable bot strength and real gem payouts.
  • New Game: Share a 4-digit lobby pin with up to 5 other players for a live round.
  • Coming Soon: Ranked ladder that mirrors SpellCast matchmaking.

SpellCast Rules

WordHex mirrors the official SpellCast ruleset from the Discord Activities wiki so the skills you build here transfer directly into the activity.

  • Play on a randomized 5×5 board and submit any word that uses adjacent tiles. Tiles reset after each word.
  • Rounds last 30 seconds; a full game is five rounds with up to six players competing asynchronously.
  • Words must be at least two letters. Six or more letters grant a +10 Spell Bonus.
  • Special tiles apply multipliers exactly like SpellCast: DL/TL for letters and DW/TW for whole words.
  • Gem tiles add to your reserve (starting at 3, capped at 10) and unlock the same spell controls.

Letter Multipliers

DL doubles the tile you land on while TL triples it. Stacks with word bonuses.

Word Multipliers

DW and TW tiles boost the total score for that submission by ×2 or ×3 respectively.

Long Word Bonus

Any six-plus letter word earns +10 points before multipliers, matching SpellCast.
